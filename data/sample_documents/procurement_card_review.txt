DOCUMENT: Review of Procurement Card Spending — Findings

This review examined 18 months of procurement card transactions across three
business units. It includes dollar amounts, vendor names, and monthly spend
tables.

Finding 1: Controls over card limits were not consistently applied.
Finding 2: Two transactions lacked supporting receipts.
Recommendation: tighten approval thresholds and require monthly reconciliation.

This is an internal review document. Findings are not yet final and have not
been shared with the affected units.
